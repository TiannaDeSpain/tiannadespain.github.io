Mayor's Choice Award Winner

By Jessica Smart

City Council member Leah Fuller (pictured left) has been nominated for this year’s The Mayor’s Choice Award Winner. In the Mayor’s speech she complimented Fuller for many things, including her dedication to her work and service to her fullest capacity. One highlight was the fundraiser she ran this year to repair Tom’s Animal Shelter that burnt down at the end of 2019. Fuller ended up raising enough money, not only to fully rebuild the shelter but also to buy all new crates and cases for the animals to reside in.

The Mayor also mentioned the close relationship Leah Fuller has nurtured with the citizens of Soda Springs over the last 3 years she has served. Many residents call on Fuller for questions regarding the current state of the town, such as inquiring about new buildings going in, crime incidents and updates on the mask mandates due to COVID-19. Overall, many citizens love Fuller, and are grateful she serves the community.
