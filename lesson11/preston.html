Christmas Concert- Still On!

By Sandra Petterson

COVID-19 has been hard on all of us this year. But, as the director of our 15th annual Christmas Concert, I am excited to announce that we have found a way for everyone to enjoy from home! With help from technology specialist Todd Gains, we will broadcast our typical light show via Facebook Live. Each of our performers this year will be pre-recording their special musical pieces from their own homes for us to play as well.

We hope that although this year has been crazy, to say the least, that we will be able to join together for this broadcast in celebration of our Lord and Savior, Jesus Christ.
